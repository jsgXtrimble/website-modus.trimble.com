Guidelines

When to use

  • In proximity to notifications or user avatars with eye-catching appeal, i.e. displaying unread messages count.
  • Displaying quick, easily digestible bits of information.

When NOT to use

  • Displaying long strings of text.
  • As a replacement for buttons or chips.

To inform judgment about color, type, space, and more
