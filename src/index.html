Design Principles

Trimble has a rich history of the application of branding to products & solutions through form, color and inspired functional consideration. Regardless of context, the application of brand details intends to create a positive emotive connection with our customers that is:

Precise

Our core visual quality and brand value that permeates every stylistic decision we make. Precise reflects our promise of accuracy and precion in every solution we deliver in all industries.

Crisp lines, structured layouts, balanced visual qualities which communicate perfection and control are essential.

Innovative

At a cultural level, the Trimble design community celebrates the consistent challenge of new solutions. We design future challenges within changing contexts and ever emerging technical posibilities.

Build upon existing solutions while contribute to the conversation allow us to be agile and relevant.

Considerate

As designers advocates and owners of a user's experience. We celebrate insight, deep understanding, and consideration for our customers. We empathize by thinking about challenges from different perspectives, and we deliver solutions that consider context of use.

Inquisitive minds, empathy and calm observation are embodied in the solutions our customers engage with.
