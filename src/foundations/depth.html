Depth

Use layering to create depth and focus the user’s attention on a task, provide temporary supplementary information, or define spatial relationships between content.

Overview

Shadows provide cues about depth, direction of movement, and surface edges. A surface’s shadow is determined by its elevation and relationship to other surfaces.

Because shadows express the degree of elevation between surfaces, they must be used consistently throughout your product.

For other elements such as cards, modals, tables etc, use one of the following classes to add depth; shadow-1, shadow-2, shadow-3, shadow-4, shadow-5
