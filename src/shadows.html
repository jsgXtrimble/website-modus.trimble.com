Shadows Depth

In the Material Design environment, virtual lights illuminate the UI. Key lights create sharper, directional shadows, called key shadows. Ambient light appears from all angles to create diffused, soft shadows, called ambient shadows.

Classes & Usage

Shadows provide cues about depth, direction of movement, and surface edges. A surfaceâ€™s shadow is determined by its elevation and relationship to other surfaces.

Because shadows express the degree of elevation between surfaces, they must be used consistently throughout your product.

For button elements, use btn-raised to add shadows. The button active/pressed state will not cast any shadows.

For other elements such as cards, modals, tables etc, use one of the following classes to add depth; shadow-1, shadow-2, shadow-3, shadow-4, shadow-5
